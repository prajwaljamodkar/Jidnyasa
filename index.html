Why Jidnyasa?

Jinyasa Concept School is dedicated to providing a joyful and stimulating learning environment for young children. With over five years of experience in educational projects, our team came together to address gaps in the current system—leading to the vision of Jinyasa Concept School.

Teaching Methods

Our experienced teachers use interactive activities, storytelling, music, art, and outdoor play to make learning fun and engaging. A low student-to-teacher ratio ensures each child gets personal attention and support.

Learning Philosophy

At Jinyasa Concept School, we believe children learn best through curiosity, creativity, and hands-on experiences. Our philosophy blends play-based, experiential, and holistic learning approaches to nurture each child's intellectual, emotional, and social development in a joyful and supportive environment.

Facilities

Our school offers safe, modern facilities with bright classrooms, age-appropriate learning materials, and a secure outdoor play area. We also feature a library, art studio, and music room to enrich every child’s learning experience.

Special Programs

Alongside our core curriculum, we offer special programs like language classes, music and movement, art workshops, cultural activities, and field trips. Regular events and celebrations help build community and create lasting memories.

Admissions

We warmly welcome new students to join our vibrant learning community. Contact us to learn more about our admission process and how to enroll your child.

Admission Process

1. Inquiry: Fill out our online inquiry form.
2. Tour: Visit the school, meet our team, and explore our environment.
3. Application: Submit the completed form and required documents.
4. Enrollment: Confirm your child’s spot upon acceptance.

Eligibility

Children must be 3 to 5 years old by September 1st of the admission year, fully potty-trained, and able to join group activities.

Required Documents

• Completed application form
• Copy of child's birth certificate
• Immunization records
• Recent photo of the child

Deadlines

Admissions are open year-round, but we recommend applying by March 1st for priority consideration. Late applications depend on availability.
